In-Person Short Course 

Power Systems and Artificial Intelligence: An Introduction

Key Course Information

  • Course fee is $1850
  • Course topics include
  • ⚑
    Leveraging multi-scale data from synchrophasors, smart meters, weather, and electricity markets
  • ⚑
    Exploring the operational carbon footprint of AI computing
  • ⚑
    Optimizing datacenter networks for energy efficiencyintegrating environmentally responsible AI solutions
  • Join a community of professionals in the electric grid and AI industries who seek to understand the latest advancements in AI, machine learning, and sustainable computing, and how these innovations will shape their work
  • Earn a certificate as you build connections with peers at the intersection of power systems and AI
