Seminars from 2025

Learning Decision-Focused Uncertainty Representations: Theory and Applications in Sustainability November 25, 2025 · SEC Room 4.405 · Christopher Yeh

Investigating Two-Stage Robust Optimization and Distributionally Robust Optimization from the Primal Perspective August 14, 2025 · SEC Room 3.314 · Bo Zeng

Bo Zeng is an associate professor at the University of Pittsburgh whose work in robust optimization has been widely applied to energy, logistics, and healthcare systems.

Randomness as a Resource: Scalable Algorithms for Grid Decision-Making August 8, 2025 · SEC Room 6.301 · Samuel Talkington

Samuel Talkington is a Georgia Tech PhD candidate whose research focuses on fast, reliable algorithms for inference and control in electric power networks.

Ignacio Aravena leads the Optimization and Control Group at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory and works at the intersection of power-system models, optimization, and high-performance computing.

Flexible Computing in Data Centers for Sustainable AI Growth April 8, 2025 · SEC Room 6.301 · Ayse K. Coskun
