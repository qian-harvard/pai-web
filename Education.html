Courses Offered at Harvard

ES 215, Electric Energy Systems

Instructor: Prof. Le Xie
This course introduces systematic modeling and control of changing electric energy systems, including smart grid & microgrid architectures for present and future systems. The foundation of high dimensional spaces and data analytical tools necessary to model and operate a modern power system will also be covered.

CS 243, ​​Advanced Computer Networks 

Instructor: Prof. Minlan Yu
This is a graduate-level course on computer networks, offering an in-depth exploration of selected advanced topics in networked systems. We will discuss the latest developments across the entire networking stack, the interactions between networks and high-level applications, and their connections with other system components such as computing and storage.
